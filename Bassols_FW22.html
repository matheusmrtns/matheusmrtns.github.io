Bassols FW22

2022 · Barcelona, Spain



Art Direction  ·  Studio Photography  ·  Retouching

Bassols is a Spanish luxury brand for bed linens and home textiles. For this project we provided the art direction of the product photos for the release of the fall/winter collection of seasonal bed linens. This included set designs for two different styles in the collection and direction of photography in two separate days.

Each of the sets were created to reflect the world in which each part of the collection exists. One would inhabit the warmest tones, focusing on yellows and teals. While another would highlight more graphic combinations of black and whites.

The warmer linens took life on a set inspired by japanese interiors, having the warm palettes of the patterns be highlighted by the natural materials in the surroundings such as fabric, wood and ceramic.

The darker tones were paired with an industrial/brutalist environment. With the set reflecting the black and white compositions of the patterns by the usage of darker sculptural and geometric furniture imposed over the white wall and concrete floor.

Each product had a variety of photos taken in set angles and zooms, going from full frame to details to be used in the brand's online store.

The variety of sets and photo angles allowed for a fluid combination of items on the collection's page on the online marketplace, creating a coherent visual for the fall/winter collection as a whole.
