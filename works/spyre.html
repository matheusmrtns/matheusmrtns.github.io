Works

Spyre

Video Game

Indie video game developed with a small team of designers. Currently between the top 10 finalists of the Playstation Talents 2020 competition. Worked in art direction, creative direction, 3D modelling, 3D animation, logo creation, level design, writing and voice acting.

The game is a first person shooter with puzzle mechanics. The objective is to escape the virtual simulated prison the main character is stuck in. The player takes on a journey going through different worlds and hacking reality alongside her drone friend.
